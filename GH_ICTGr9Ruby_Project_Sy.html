WAYS TO PERVENT FIRE!

WARNING!

Warning, this is NOT a way to stop fire immediately, for that information Visit our second Website!

UNLPUG YOUR WIRES!

It can happen due to loose or corroded wires, wetness, or unplugging something from an overloaded outlet, and may even result in a fire.

Own a FIRE EXTINGUISHER!

Keep at least 1 fire extinguisher in a central location on each floor of your home. This includes the basement if you have one. Mount them at least 4–5 feet from the floor in plain view so time isn't wasted looking for one.

STORE FLAMMABLE THINGS CORRECTLY

Store flammable liquids in an approved flammable storage cabinet. A variety of commercially manufactured cabinets are available. Cabinets must be listed with UL1275 and labeled "Flammable – Keep Fire Away". Doors must be well fitted, self-closing and equipped with a 3-point latch kit system.
